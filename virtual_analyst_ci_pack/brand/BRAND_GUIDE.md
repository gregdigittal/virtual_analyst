# Virtual Analyst — CI / Brand Guide (Option B: AI Lab / High-Tech)
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-02-14

## Brand essence
- **Modern analyst workstation**: fast, precise, technical.
- **Governed AI**: AI assists; outputs are defendable.
- **Signal over noise**: strong hierarchy, minimal chrome.

## Voice & tone
- Confident, concise, evidence-first.
- Avoid hype; prefer “draft → verify → commit”.

## Colors
Primary:
- Midnight `#0B1020` (background)
- Electric Blue `#3B82F6` (primary)
- Violet `#7C3AED` (selected/committed states)
- Magenta `#EC4899` (risk emphasis)

Neutrals:
- Text `#E5E7EB`, Secondary `#B6C2D1`, Border `#22304F`

Semantic:
- Success `#22C55E`, Warning `#F59E0B`, Danger `#EF4444`

## Typography
- Headings: **Sora**
- Body/UI: **Inter**
- Numbers/Tables: **JetBrains Mono**

## Shapes & elevation
- Card radius: 18px
- Input radius: 6px
- Glow reserved for key states/primary CTA.

## Signature UI motifs
- Draft → Commit compiler moment (validation checks)
- Evidence panel (source chips + confidence)
- Monte Carlo bands: P10/P50/P90 = Blue/Violet/Magenta
