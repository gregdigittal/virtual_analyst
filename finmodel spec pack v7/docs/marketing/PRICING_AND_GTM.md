# Pricing & Go-to-Market — FinModel (working title)
**Date:** 2026-02-07

## Pricing principles
- Simple: **base subscription + seats + usage bundles**
- Meter what costs: **LLM tokens**, **PDF pages/OCR**, **Monte Carlo compute**, **storage**, **ERP sync volume**
- Enterprise-ready: annual contracts, SSO, audit logs, BYO-LLM key option

## Suggested tiers (illustrative USD; tune after pilots)

### Starter (SMB / single-entity CFO) — $149–$299 / month
- 1 entity, 1 venture, basic scenarios
- 1 ERP connector (Xero/QBO)
- Small included LLM token bundle; overage available
- Excel export (static), no bidirectional links

### Professional (Analyst / growth / boutiques) — $499–$999 / month
- Multi-entity (e.g., up to 5), higher run limits
- PDF spreading + review loop
- Monte Carlo enabled (capped)
- **Excel bidirectional live links**
- Memo packs (IC / valuation note)
- 3–10 seats included; extra seats priced

### Enterprise (Banks / PE / listed-equity teams) — $2,500–$10,000+ / month (annual preferred)
- High caps / unlimited options
- Multi-currency + NCI + country risk overlays
- Higher MC limits + priority routing policies
- SSO/SAML, audit export, dedicated environments (optional)
- Multiple ERP connectors + higher sync frequency
- SLA + onboarding

## Usage-based components (all tiers)
- **LLM tokens**: included bundle + overage per 1M tokens
- **Extraction**: included PDF pages + overage per 1k pages (if OCR-heavy)
- **Monte Carlo compute**: included runs/sims + overage per heavy run or per 1k sims
- **Storage**: included GB-month + overage

## Packaging options
- **Managed LLM**: you route + meter and charge usage
- **BYO LLM key**: customer provides keys; you charge platform only (good for enterprise procurement)

## ICP-specific positioning & offers

### Boutique advisory / valuation firms
- Hook: “Statements → model → memo in hours, not days.”
- Offer: Pro plan with discounted annual + onboarding pack.

### Growth company CFO/FP&A
- Hook: “Funding needs & runway under downside cases; ERP sync.”
- Offer: Starter trial → Pro upgrade for MC + Excel live links.

### Credit teams / lenders
- Hook: “Spreading + ratios + covenant headroom + credit memo packs.”
- Offer: Enterprise pilot with success criteria.

### PE / buy-side / equity research
- Hook: “Evidence-backed assumptions + comps + valuation + Excel-native workflow.”
- Offer: Enterprise annual; add data integrations later.

## Go-to-market (first 90 days)
1) Founder-led demos to 30–50 boutique firms and CFO networks
2) Launch “industry driver templates” as lead magnets
3) Partnerships: accounting firms, CFO-as-a-service, Xero ecosystem partners
4) Publish 3 demo videos:
   - PDF spread + review
   - Draft → commit and governance
   - Excel push/pull and memo pack generation

## Messaging pillars
1) Trust & auditability (compile-to-config, evidence trace, change logs)
2) Speed (spreading + refresh + memo automation)
3) Excel-native (meet analysts where they work)
4) Cost control (routing + metering + plan limits)

## KPI targets (early)
- Time-to-first-model < 60 minutes
- Trial→paid conversion > 15%
- Gross margin > 70% with routing policies and bundles
